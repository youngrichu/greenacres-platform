# Buyer Portal Guide

The Buyer Portal is where coffee importers and roasters can browse our premium Ethiopian coffee offerings and submit sample requests.

## Getting Started

### 1. Registration

New users can register by clicking **"Sign Up"** on the navigation bar.

**Required Information:**
- Full Name
- Email Address
- Password (minimum 8 characters)
- Company Name (optional)

## Features

### Coffee Catalog

Browse our complete catalog of premium Ethiopian coffees with advanced filtering:

- **Origin Region**: Sidamo, Yirgacheffe, Guji, Harrar, Limu
- **Processing Method**: Washed, Natural, Honey
- **Grade**: Grade 1, Grade 2, Specialty

![Coffee Catalog](/screenshots/portal-catalog.png)

### Coffee Details

Each coffee listing includes:
- **Flavor Profile**: Cupping notes and tasting descriptors
- **Farm Information**: Altitude, varietal, harvest season
- **Pricing**: FOB pricing per kilogram
- **Certifications**: Organic, Fair Trade, Rainforest Alliance

![Coffee Detail Page](/screenshots/portal-coffee-detail.png)

### Sample Requests (Inquiries)

Submit an inquiry to request samples or quotes:

1. Navigate to a coffee detail page
2. Click **"Request Sample"**
3. Fill in the inquiry form:
   - Quantity (in kg)
   - Target shipment date
   - Additional notes
4. Submit and track your inquiry in **"My Inquiries"**

## My Inquiries

View all your submitted inquiries with status tracking:

| Status | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Pending** | Inquiry submitted, awaiting review |
| **Reviewed** | Our team is preparing a response |
| **Quoted** | Price quote has been sent |
| **Completed** | Transaction finalized |
