## Key Features

### Premium Coffee Collection
Browse our meticulously curated selection of Ethiopia's finest single-origin specialty coffees. Our catalog provides detailed flavor profiles, cupping notes, and technical specifications for every lot.

![Our Coffee Collection](/media/catalog_current.png)

### Direct Inquiry System
Buyers can submit digital inquiries for specific lots, request samples, and communicate directly with our export team. Track the status of your requests from submission to final offer.

### Efficient Administration
For internal teams, our dashboard provides a centralized hub to manage the entire coffee catalog in real-time. Update prices, manage visibility, and respond to incoming buyer inquiries with ease.
